Volunteering at TAC Boxing

Tulare Athletic Boxing Club is based out of Tulare CA which is where I was raised. This is a non profit organization and their main focus is boxing, they have free boxing class that anyone can take Monday through Thursday from 6pm to 8pm. This is a place that help kids stay off the streets and better use it learning a new skill. They also provide other service to different parts of the community. For a couple years we provided hot meals provided by Fugazis, to the homeless or anyone that wanted one. During the holidays they provided clothing, food and gifts to anyone in the community that decided to part take and throughout the year they bring the clinic in a bus and provide free basic medical needs. And for the kids that are regulars and want to start their amateur careers, they purchase you a license, pay for your physical and put you in boxing matches that take place all over California. They also provide free transportation there and back to Tulare. This is an organization I am proud to be a part of, as of recently all I do is maintain their website but in the past I did it all and I still try to make it to some of the occasions today.
