Volunteering at Art Produce

Art Produce is a place in NorthPark, in San Diego, that is involved with the Karen people who are refugees from Myanmar. They use art to communicate with refugees who do not know English and to get to know them a little more and have them express themselves through art. There is a big community of Karen and I met many when I volunteered here. We would sit down and paint while we tried to communicate with a person from Karen or Sudan who did not, or barley spoke English. There I met many wonderful people who had sad stories of how they were kicked out of the only place they knew as home. At the end of the year they hosted an art show and showcased everyone work. They also have workshops where they help the elderly Karen people learn to speak English and to help them feel a little more at home. The children also have their workshops where they also get taught English and also how to just live in the USA.
