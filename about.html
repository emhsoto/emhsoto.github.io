ABOUT ME

I like to take walks to clearance sections at stores. I like to buy use and resell items. I am always looking for a bargain. I consider myself to be frugal and cheap. I am team iOS, but also team Windows OS. Spanish is my first language and I am a first generation Mexican American and student. I want to be a role model to other young Latinos who come from a similar background and show them that there is a world outside of Tulare County and that pursing higher education can open the door to endless opportunities. I am politically active and am passionate about policy that affects minority communities. Please VOTE in November and every election for that matter. Now I will leave you with one of my favorite quotes by Lao Tzu:

“He/She who knows he/she is has enough, is rich”
