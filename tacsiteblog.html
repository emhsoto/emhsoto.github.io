Tulare Athletic Boxing Club Website

Tacboxing.com is another one that I have mostly finished. This is just a very simple, informative site about the organizations boxing events and free trainings throughout the year. This one has a similar concept as my first one, but this has a lot less work put into it. This has a couple of pages one that has a calendar and shows you when there is trainings and boxing events, there is a donation page were I use plug in that allows users to leave a donation using a credit card or PayPal an then there is a form that you can fill out to volunteer your time with events held throughout the year. This site is one I need to get back too and spend some more time on and I will do that after taking these courses.
