Volunteering at YALLA

YALLA is an organization based in San Diego and they work with student refugees from countries around the world. The biggest ethnic group represented there is from Iraq and next would be Syria, there are also kids from Sudan, Guatemala, Afghanistan, and Burma. Their goal for these kids is to go on to college and to help them assimilate to their new homes. They use soccer to bring everyone together and boy do they love soccer. I have never met bigger soccer fanatics than these kids. They played every day at school and after school for leagues and many of them are very good. I helped them with tutoring and I also played soccer with them and me not being a soccer player I was getting schooled by the first graders. I learned more form this experience then I could ever be of help to them and I just hope that I could be one small part of their journeys to feel more comfortable being in here in a foreign land that they do not know and help them feel like home here in America.
